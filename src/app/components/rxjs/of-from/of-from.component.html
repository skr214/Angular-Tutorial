Of & From


of: Creates an Observable that emits the values you provide as arguments, one by one, in the order they are given.Useful when you want to turn a list of values into a stream of emissions.
Example: of(1, 2, 3) → emits 1, 2, 3.


from: Creates an Observable from an array, promise, iterable, or another Observable. Useful when you want to convert existing data structures or async results into a stream.
Example: from([10, 20, 30]) → emits 10, 20, 30.
